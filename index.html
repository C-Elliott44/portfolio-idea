ABOUT ME

I AM A DEVELOPER WHO LOVE TO TINKER WITH FRONT-END BUT ALSO GETS A KICK OUT OF WIRING UP THE BACK-END.

I'm a naturally curiuos person who love to play the "What if I do this?" game with my code. I have a drive passion for learning and love making engaging, purpose oriented, and functional web applications that have a simple and clean design.

I have built web applications using popular JQuery, React, Node.js, to name a few, and have a strong foundation HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I've recently completed UCSD Extention's Full Stack 12 Week extensive bootcamp.

Before starting development, I worked in the BioTech industry, where I was the Logistic/Shipping Manager.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy spending my time outdoors surfing, hiking, and having bonfire with friends and family at our local beaches.
